The Site

This site is a resource hub for the philosophy of religion. Its aims are two-fold: 1) to introduce the main concepts, topcs, problems, and thinkers in the philosophy of religion and 2) to provide links to other resources in the philosophy of religion.

The site is intended to be interesting and engaging to anyone regardless of their religious or non-religious background. Thus, I have included material representing diverse viewpoints, including some secular viewpoints. The site does focus more, however, on theism, or the Christian, Islamic, and Jewish belief in one God. This represents both the historical tendency in the academic discipline of philosophy of religion to focus on theism and the Christian background of the website's creator.

Site Designer and Developer: Silas Langley
